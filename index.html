A Collector's Dream: The Top 5 Olivia Rodrigo Vinyl Records



Olivia Rodrigo, the singing sensation behind chart-topping albums like "SOUR," has captured the hearts of fans around the world. For avid music enthusiasts and collectors, an Olivia Rodrigo vinyl record is the ultimate treasure. In this article, we'll explore the top 5 Olivia Rodrigo vinyl records available, celebrating the unparalleled sound quality and the enchanting charm of vinyl. We'll also discuss how these vinyl records can be paired with Olivia Rodrigo merch for an immersive fan experience. 

"SOUR" Vinyl LP

Olivia Rodrigo's debut album "SOUR" was a major hit, and it's now available on vinyl. This record offers an incredible listening experience with its warm, analog sound. The vinyl version includes all the beloved tracks like "Drivers License," "Good 4 U," and more. It's the perfect addition to any collector's library of vinyl records. 

"SOUR" Deluxe Edition Vinyl

For true fans, the "SOUR" Deluxe Edition Vinyl is a must-have. This edition includes not only the original tracks but also exclusive songs and demos that provide a deeper insight into Olivia's creative process. It's a delightful addition to your Olivia Rodrigo vinyl record collection. 

"Drivers License" 7-inch Vinyl Single

Olivia Rodrigo's breakthrough hit, "Drivers License," is now available as a 7-inch vinyl single. This is a charming collector's item, featuring both the hit single and the song "deja vu" on the B-side. The vinyl single is perfect for reliving the emotions of this iconic track.

"deja vu" 7-inch Vinyl Single

For fans who can't get enough of "deja vu," this 7-inch vinyl single is a must-own. The single features the song itself, as well as "good 4 u" on the B-side. It's a compact yet cherished addition to your collection of Olivia Rodrigo vinyl records. 

"SOUR" Picture Disc Vinyl

The "SOUR" Picture Disc Vinyl is not only an auditory delight but a visual one as well. This record features an image of Olivia Rodrigo on the disc itself, adding a unique and artistic touch to your collection. It's a standout piece among Olivia Rodrigo vinyl records.
